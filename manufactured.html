How To Use

  • Click/tap on "Materials" to toggle the material filters
  • Uncheck each material as you fill up on them
  • The associated allegiance/state will hide once you no longer need it
  • Under each superpower is listed the states to focus on
  • If you don't need any state under a superpower, the entire superpower will hide
